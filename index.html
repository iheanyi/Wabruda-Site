The Wabruda Mission Statement

The Wabruda is an organization based on brotherhood amongst African Americans. Through intellectual elevation, communal interaction and faith in God, we will strive to achieve social awareness and personal enligthnment.

What we do: Challenge ourselves.

Every Sunday, we meet as a group to discuss topics that range from current events, spirituality, relationship to that new single from that artist that you like. Throughout every discussion, we aim to enrich our knowledge, develop critical thinking skills, and to quote our advisor Dr. D, "Become critical consumers of our environment."

Who can join: Every man on campus

There is this common misconception that only African Americans can join the group. That is not true. We invite everyone to come and sit in one of our meetings to see what we are all about. Your presence means a lot to us.
We hope that our interaction is mutually enrenching, intellectually, socially and spiritually and helps us gain deeper appreciation of our similarities but also our differences

So join us

Whether it is at a Wabruda sponsored event, one of our weekly meeting come and hangout with us. Let us talk, enrich ourselves and have fun
